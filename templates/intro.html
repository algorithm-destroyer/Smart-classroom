About Our Application

Smart Classroom is designed to create an easy accessible learning platform for students. Our mission is to break down barriers to education by providing a comprehensive digital environment that supports both students and educators.

With our platform, students can access learning materials anytime, anywhere, while educators can efficiently manage classes, track attendance, and provide personalized learning experiences. We believe that technology should enhance education, not complicate it.

Our application is built with accessibility in mind, ensuring that all students, regardless of their circumstances, can benefit from quality education. We are committed to continuous improvement and innovation to meet the evolving needs of the educational landscape.

Our Features

Recorded Classes

Access all your recorded lectures anytime. Never miss important content with our comprehensive class recording system.

Recorded Notes

Automatically generated notes from lectures. Highlight important points and add your own annotations for better retention.

Speech to Text

Convert lectures to text in real-time. Perfect for students with different learning preferences and accessibility needs.

Online Attendance

Mark attendance with location verification. Ensures students are present in class while maintaining accurate records.

AI Timetable Scheduler

Intelligent scheduling that considers all constraints. Optimize class timings for both students and teachers.

Rescheduling

Flexible rescheduling based on constraints. Easily adjust timetables when unexpected changes occur.
